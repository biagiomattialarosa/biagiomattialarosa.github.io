I'm mainly interested in the development of methodologies to improve the explainability or
interpretability
of machine learning approaches, in particular for Deep Neural Networks.
Specifically, my current works are closely related to the following domains:
- Multi-hop reasoning.
- Memory-Augmented Neural Networks.
- Neural-Symbolic Approaches. 

I would also very happy to work, deepening my knowledge on them, on:
- Evolutionary Deep Learning.
- Natural Language Understanding.
- Bio-Inspired Architectures.

If you want to collaborate with me and you are a graduate student or a phd student, please send me an email with the subject [collaboration], indicating your advisor/laboratory. Usually, my response time is less than 1 day.
